## X/Twitter Posting Tips

- **Hook format:** Lead with a pain point or surprising stat, not the product
- **Thread > single tweet** for comparison/review content
- **Visual proof** wins: screenshots, before/after, setup photos
- **Urgency works:** "Deal ends today" + link converts 2-3x higher
- **Don't hard sell every tweet:** 80% value, 20% affiliate
- **Best posting times:** 8-10am and 12-1pm (engagement peaks)
- **Hashtags:** #TechDeals #OnlineLearning #MacApps #VPN #WebHosting
