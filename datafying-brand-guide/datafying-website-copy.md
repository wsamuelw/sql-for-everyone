## POSITIONING

**Core positioning:**
> No jargon, no decks, no hand-offs.

The approach:
- **Show, don't tell** — the voice proves the positioning, not labels
- **One founder** — strategic thinking + hands-on, same person
- **Direct, specific, human** — sounds like a conversation, not a pitch deck

Two ways to work together:
1. **Advisory call** — think it through (30-60 min)
2. **Analysis sprint** — find the answer (2-4 weeks)

## RESULTS SECTION

**Heading:** Capabilities that move the needle

**Result 1: Semantic Layer**
> **Your Dashboards Disagree. Your Team Pays the Price.**
> Every department built its own version of the truth. Now nobody trusts any of them — and decisions stall while people argue over numbers.
> A unified semantic layer in Snowflake. One definition, one source, shared across every team.
> Trust in the numbers returns. Arguments about them stop.

**Result 2: K-Means Clustering**
> **"High Value" Is Not a Strategy**
> You call a segment "high value" because someone said so three years ago. Nobody's checked if those customers still behave that way.
> Ran clustering on live transactional and behavioural data. Found the segments that reflect how people actually act today.
> Targeting based on reality. Results that reflect it.

**Result 3: AI Adoption**
> **Your Best People Are Doing a Robot's Job**
> Smart people spend hours every month on copy-paste reporting. They didn't sign up for this — and you're paying senior salaries for it.
> Built an AI workflow that replaces the manual loop entirely. Not automating steps — eliminating them.
> Ten days of tedium, reduced to one day of decisions.
