A note from the founder

I spent years inside data teams at some of Australia's biggest brands. Here's what I wish someone had told me.

Most data teams solve yesterday's question. They build dashboards that tell you what happened, not what to do next. The real unlock isn't more data — it's a sharper question and someone who can find the answer.

That's why I started Datafying. Not to build another dashboard. To sit with founders, CMOs, and CEOs who know there's more in their data — and help them find it.

Sometimes that's a 30-minute call where we think through a problem together. Sometimes it's a three-week sprint where I dig in, build the model, and hand you the decision. Either way, you get me — not a junior, not an account manager.

Samuel Wong · Founder, Datafying · Previously at one of Australia's leading loyalty programs, a major entertainment resort, and one of the largest supermarket retailers.
