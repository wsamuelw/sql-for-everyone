### Task 8: Draft First 3 Content Posts

**What:** Write 3 LinkedIn posts — one from each of the first 3 content pillars — to have ready when the profile goes live.

- [ ] **Step 1: Draft Post 1 — Practical data/AI insight**

Topic: Something you've actually done. Examples:
- "Here's what I learned integrating LLMs into a data workflow"
- "How I cut reporting turnaround by 60% for a retail SME"
- "What changed when I moved from dashboards to self-serve analytics"

Write 150-300 words. First person. Short paragraphs. Lead with the problem, end with the lesson. No jargon.

- [ ] **Step 2: Draft Post 2 — Founder/leader perspective**

Topic: Something founders get wrong about data. Examples:
- "The biggest mistake I see founders make with their data"
- "What I wish every CMO knew about customer analytics"
- "You don't need a full-time Head of Data. You need a plan."

Write 150-300 words. Speak directly to the founder/leader audience. End with a question that invites real conversation (not "Do you agree?").

- [ ] **Step 3: Draft Post 3 — Mentoring/career thread**

Topic: Advice for data professionals. Examples:
- "What I tell every junior analyst on day one"
- "The skill that separates good analysts from great ones"
- "Why I mentor across 4 universities (and what I learn from it)"

Write 150-300 words. Conversational, generous, specific. This serves mentees and builds authority.

- [ ] **Step 4: Save drafts**

Save all 3 drafts in a notes app or doc. You'll post them over the first week after the profile goes live — one every 2-3 days.

**What:** After 2 weeks of posting and engaging, review what's working and adjust.

- [ ] **Step 1: Check profile views**

Go to LinkedIn Analytics. Note:
- Total profile views (compared to previous 2 weeks)
- Who's viewing (recruiters? founders? data people?)
- Which sections they're looking at

- [ ] **Step 2: Check post performance**

Review the 3+ posts you've published:
- Which got the most engagement?
- Which attracted the right audience (founders, recruiters, mentees)?
- What topics resonated?

- [ ] **Step 3: Adjust content strategy**

Based on what's working:
- Double down on the pillar that's getting traction
- Adjust posting cadence if needed (more or less frequent)
- Note any new content ideas that came from engagement

- [ ] **Step 4: Update Featured section if needed**

If any posts performed well, add them to Featured. Replace weaker items.
