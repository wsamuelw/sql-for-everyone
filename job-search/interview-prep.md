# Interview Talking Points

When the user asks for interview prep, personalise these core stories for the specific role.

## Core Stories

- **$500M Blackstone investment**: Crown CX analytics informing equity investment decisions across the business
- **Flybuys retail media Greenfield**: Co-creating the data strategy roadmap for a new revenue stream from scratch
- **Crown CX capability build**: Built a functional analytics capability from scratch, embedding data-driven culture
- **Partner revenue growth (80%)**: Measurement frameworks and domain knowledge driving partner-sourced revenue at Flybuys
- **Self-serve analytics transformation**: Shifting organisations from manual reporting to self-serve (Crown, Flybuys, datafying)
- **Team building**: Led 10+ at Crown, 20+ at Flybuys, developed 10+ emerging professionals at datafying

## How to Use

Tailor which stories to lead with based on what the role emphasises:
- **Leadership roles**: Crown capability build, team building, $500M Blackstone
- **Data science roles**: Flybuys Greenfield, partner revenue growth, predictive modelling
- **Analytics/BI roles**: Self-serve transformation, measurement frameworks, stakeholder engagement
