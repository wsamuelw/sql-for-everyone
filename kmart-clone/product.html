Meet Daniel, The Bot! 🤖

Looking for the perfect little brother who never leaves a mess, never argues, and always gets good grades? Meet Daniel Wong — the world's first robot brother who actually listens!

Daniel is what every parent dreams of. He's the good boy who always tidies up after himself. Toys? Put away. Plates? Rinsed and stacked. Bed? Made before 7am. No nagging required — he just does it. Every. Single. Time.

But wait, there's more! Daniel is an A+ student. He studies every night, finishes his homework before dinner, and actually enjoys learning new things. Math problems? Solved. Spelling tests? Perfect score. Science projects? He built a volcano that actually worked. (Unlike certain humans in the house.)

Need someone to feed themselves? Daniel eats by himself, no fuss, no spills, no "I don't like broccoli" tantrums. He even says "please" and "thank you" — unprompted. Wild, right?

And here's the best part: Daniel never hits, kicks, or fights with his sister. In fact, he's the best big brother ever. He shares, takes turns, and even helps with her homework. If only every sibling could be this chill.

Oh, and did we mention he's super smart? Daniel can speak 12 languages, do mental math faster than a calculator, and remember every fact he's ever learned. He's basically a walking encyclopedia — but way more fun.
