## About

Most founders I talk to have the same problem: data sitting in spreadsheets or siloed tools, a team that's underwater, and dashboards nobody looks at. They know they should be making better decisions with their data — they just don't have a full-time Head of Data on payroll, and they don't need one.

That's what I do. I step in as your fractional Head of Data — setting direction, building capability, and getting your team to self-serve. I'm not here to sell you a dashboard. I'm here to make sure the decisions you make with your data actually move the needle.

I've spent 12 years inside the data trenches at Crown Resorts (multi-property, C-suite, $500M CX strategy) and Flybuys (9M+ members, retail media). Now I bring that enterprise-grade rigour to 10+ SMEs across Melbourne who can't afford — or don't need — a full-time analytics team.

I also mentor emerging data professionals across Melbourne University, UNSW, RMIT, and Monash. It's not a side project — it's how I stay connected to what good data culture actually looks like at ground level.
