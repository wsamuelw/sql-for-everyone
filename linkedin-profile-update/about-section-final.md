## About Section

Most data work ends up ignored. I take full ownership so it doesn't.

Whatever outcome I help analyse or measure, I treat it like my own business. That's why I partner with senior leaders whose KPIs are directly linked to the outcomes — because when we're one team driving the same direction, decisions actually happen.

I've spent 15 years making this work at scale. At Crown Resorts, I influenced a $500M capex investment decision by presenting key driver analysis to two different CEOs — one wanted a technical deep dive, the other needed high-level impact to allocate resources. Same analysis, tailored for each audience. At Flybuys, I drove $3M+ in incremental sales uplift for Coles through test-and-control experimentation across 20+ weekly campaigns targeting 9M active members.

The last 18 months, I've gone all-in on AI. I built a no-code end-to-end workflow in Claude Code that takes data from raw to insights to presentation in under a day — a process that used to take weeks. Human in the loop at every step, because trust is everything when using AI. I've also built Claude Code skills for market research (synthetic personas for rapid validation) and A/B testing (executive-ready one-pagers). I use these tools daily. This isn't a course I took — it's how I work now.

My stack: SQL, R, Python, Claude Code, Codex, Ollama for local models, GitHub Actions, Vercel. AI-native, not AI-curious.

I also mentor emerging data professionals across Melbourne University, Monash, RMIT, UNSW, and DVS (US). My mentees now work at PwC, AGL, and other large organisations. I've been a guest speaker at Monash for 50 students and share data and AI insights with 7,000+ followers on X.

I'm now looking for a senior analytics or data leadership role at a tech-forward company. If you need someone who takes full ownership and can both set the strategy and do the work, let's talk.
