### Task 8: Draft First 3 Content Posts

**What:** Write 3 LinkedIn posts — one from each of the first 3 content pillars — to have ready when the profile goes live.

- [ ] **Step 1: Draft Post 1 — AI-native analytics**

Topic: Something you've actually built with AI. Examples:
- "Here's how I'm using Claude Code in my analytics workflow"
- "What I learned building LLM-powered data pipelines"
- "The AI tools that actually changed how I work (and the ones that didn't)"

Write 150-300 words. First person. Short paragraphs. Lead with the problem, end with the lesson. Show technical depth without jargon.

- [ ] **Step 2: Draft Post 2 — Enterprise analytics at scale**

Topic: Something you learned operating at scale. Examples:
- "What managing analytics for 9M+ members taught me"
- "The biggest mistake I see in CX analytics"
- "Why most analytics platforms fail (and what I did differently at Crown)"

Write 150-300 words. Speak to the tech audience. Show you've operated at their scale. End with an insight that applies beyond your specific experience.

- [ ] **Step 3: Draft Post 3 — Super IC / leadership**

Topic: Your take on the future of analytics. Examples:
- "Why I believe the super IC is the future of analytics"
- "What I tell every junior analyst about the AI shift"
- "The skill that separates good analysts from great ones in the age of AI"

Write 150-300 words. Forward-looking, generous, specific. This serves tech recruiters (shows you think about the craft) and mentees (shows you invest in people).

- [ ] **Step 4: Save drafts**

Save all 3 drafts in a notes app or doc. You'll post them over the first week after the profile goes live — one every 2-3 days.
